List of Bosses

Disclaimer: Bosses can be challenged in any order or even skiped. Some bosses however need to be defeated in order to advance the game or to challenge other bosses

Pre-Hardmode Bosses


King Slime

Not required to progress the game


Eye of Cthulhu

Not required to progress the game


Eater of Worlds or Brain of Cthulhu

Not required to progress the game


Queen Bee

Not required to progress the game


Skeletron

Required to be able to enter the dungeon but not to progress the game


Deerclops

Not required to progress the game


Wall of Flesh

Required to progress the game. Required to be defeated to turn world into "hardmode."


Hardmode Bosses


Queen Slime

Not required to progress the game


The Twins

Required to be defeated before challenging Plantera


The Destroyer

Required to be defeated before challenging Plantera


Skeletron Prime

Required to be defeated before challenging Plantera


Plantera

Required to progress the game. Required to be defeated before challenging Golem


Golem

Required to progress the game. Required to be defeated in order to challege Lunatic Cultist


Empress of Light

Not required to progress the game


Duke Fishron

Not required to progress the game


Lunatic Cultist

Required to progress the game. Required to be defeated in order to challenge Moon Lord


Moon Lord

Final Boss of the game
